Maintain Consistency

Consistency builds familiarity and reduces cognitive load

DO

  • Use consistent terminology throughout
  • Maintain visual consistency (colors, icons, spacing)
  • Keep interaction patterns predictable
  • Follow platform conventions when appropriate
  • Establish and follow a design system

DON'T

  • Mix different design styles or themes
  • Change terminology for the same concept
  • Place similar functions in different locations
  • Use different patterns for similar interactions
  • Introduce unnecessary novelty in basic functions
