Gesture Design Implementation

Platform-specific considerations and real-world applications

Gesture Accessibility Considerations

Motor Impairments

  • Provide alternative interaction methods (buttons)
  • Allow gesture customization when possible
  • Implement larger touch targets (min 48Ã—48dp)
  • Reduce gesture complexity and precision requirements

Visual Impairments

  • Ensure all gesture-based actions have clear visual cues
  • Provide text alternatives for gesture-based interactions
  • Support screen readers with proper gesture descriptions
  • Design for high contrast and visible focus states

Real-World Gesture Implementations

Photo Gallery

  • Swipe horizontally to navigate between photos
  • Pinch to zoom in/out on images
  • Double-tap to quick zoom
  • Long press to save or share

Messaging

  • Swipe left to reveal reply/forward options
  • Swipe right to reveal delete
  • Long press to select multiple messages
  • Pull down to refresh conversation
